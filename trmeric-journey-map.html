Discussion Notes
Open question

Where does discovery really start for a customer who knows they need help but does not yet know how to frame the work?

Trust question

If the early flow feels generic, both provider confidence and customer confidence drop fast.

Design implication

The product has to keep being useful after matching. Otherwise it feels thin the moment real work begins.

About

I specialize in taking digital products from first hypothesis through scale

Product Manager and 2026 Rice MBA candidate. Ten years delivering consumer-facing digital products across healthcare, wellness, and financial services, in both early-stage startups and large enterprise consultancy.

I work across the full product lifecycle: research, roadmap, delivery, and iteration. In regulated markets, that means every UX call has compliance implications, every sprint has stakeholder visibility, and every research finding needs to come with a recommendation, not just a summary.

What I bring
Research that drives roadmap decisions
I plan and run usability research, synthesize findings, and present recommendations to stakeholders with business impact rationale. Every roadmap decision has evidence behind it.
Competitive analysis before product direction
I benchmark digital product landscapes before defining what to build. Competitive analyses I've run have shaped investment cases, go-to-market strategies, and feature prioritization across multiple engagements.
Agile delivery in complex, regulated domains
Healthcare, insurance, entertainment union contexts. Distributed teams, compliance requirements, and competing stakeholder priorities all in the same sprint. I've managed that environment across multiple 0-to-1 builds.
